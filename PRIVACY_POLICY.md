## 2. LMSA Privacy Overview

### LMSA Android App

Minimal collection, maximum privacy:

- **Local Vault:** Your characters and chat history are stored in a local JSON file on your Android device.
- **Direct Handshake:** For LM Studio and Ollama, the app talks directly to your computer. Data never touches TechMitten servers.

### ⚠️ Network Security Disclosure

LM Studio and Ollama do not currently support native encryption (HTTPS) for local connections. Data transmitted over your local Wi-Fi is unencrypted and could potentially be intercepted by sophisticated actors on the same network. We recommend using LMSA on trusted, private networks.

### 4.2 Data Subject Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or any other jurisdiction where the GDPR applies, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of Access (Article 15):** You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and to request access to that data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

- **Right to Rectification (Article 16):** You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Article 17):** Under certain circumstances, you can request the deletion of your personal data. However, we may be required to retain data where lawfully obligated to do so.

- **Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18):** You can request that we limit the processing of your personal data in specific circumstances, such as while we verify its accuracy.

- **Right to Data Portability (Article 20):** You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit it to another controller where technically feasible.

- **Right to Object (Article 21):** You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, including for marketing purposes or based on legitimate interests.

- **Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22):** You have rights regarding processing that produces legal or similarly significant effects based solely on automated decision-making or profiling. While our services involve minimal automated decision-making, we do not use profiling that has legal effects on you.

- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Where we rely on consent to process your personal data, you can withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing before consent was withdrawn.

## 9. Optional Web Search (Powered by Brave Search)

LMSA offers an optional "Web Search" feature powered by **Brave Search**. When enabled, this feature sends your search queries to the Brave Search API to fetch real-time context from the live web. This improves the accuracy of AI responses while maintaining a high standard of privacy.

- **Opt-in only:** Web Search is disabled by default and must be explicitly enabled in the app settings. You may disable it at any time.

- **Privacy-First results:** Brave Search is designed to be privacy-respecting and does not track users, build search profiles, or sell your data. However, using this feature does transmit your search terms outside your local network.

- **What is sent:** When enabled, only the specific search query generated by the AI (based on your prompt) is sent to the Brave Search API. No personally identifiable information is attached to these requests by LMSA.

- **Third-party processing:** Brave processes your queries according to their own [Privacy Policy](https://brave.com/privacy/browser/#search). Brave Search is known for its commitment to anonymity and not tracking users.

- **Data security:** All requests to Brave Search are transmitted over secure, encrypted HTTPS channels.

- **Consent:** By enabling Web Search, you consent to the transmission of your AI-generated search queries to Brave Search for the purpose of retrieving live information.

- **No guarantee of accuracy:** While Brave Search provides high-quality results, we do not guarantee the absolute accuracy or safety of information returned from the live web.
